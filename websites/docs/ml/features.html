Features
This modified version of Action Quake 2, has some new features/modifications like..

gl_modulate autokicking with to high values.

M4-Script detection logging/autokicking.

Location files, %L & %S in say/say_team messages.

Message to all players when a player has been kicked for teamkilling

A stuff command for the rcon user, to stuff actions/messages to a player, team or all.

Removed damage added, for Teamkilling and after round is over, to get a more accurate reading out of damage you've caused the other team.

Plus all the stuff, i've forgot to mention here.
