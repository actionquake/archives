About Lowlag AQ2

When you fire a weapon, any weapon, the original 1.52 code actually plays TWO sounds for that shot, generating a lot of network traffic that bogs down a server in a firefight. What this modification does is use a Q2 built-in macro to play the weapon sounds. Unfortunately for you, this requires a small modification of your own to work. Either you need to download a small client pack, or you need to rename your sounds as below. For those of you with the AQ:Espionage client pack, you already have these sounds in the proper places.
