Action Quake:Edition New Sounds Information

HOW TO USE THE SOUNDS
---------------------

1. Create a dir named "user" within your action/sound directory.
2. Copy all sounds to this directory.
3. Copy sndlist.ini into your action directory.
   (if you already use a sndlist.ini, add all new sounds to it)
4. Enable download options for clients.
5. Let your clients know that new sounds are available.

Done! :)
