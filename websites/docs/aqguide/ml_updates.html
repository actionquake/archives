Updates
Here i'll write information about what's been fixed or added in diffrent versions of ACTION ML.

ML 3.25
No major changes since ML 3.24g, but this is the first public release.

ML 3.24g
Tweeked the timing for server/client checks, to reduce overflows.

If you keep overflowing all the time, i'd suggest you check your rate and your cl_maxfps, with a low rate you can't have a to high cl_maxfps. a tip might be to lower your cl_maxfps down to 20-30, when you join to see if that lowers your overflows (and lag). And then try and increase the value if you need too.

ML 3.24f
Added 2 more detection checks for gl_modulate.
Modified server/client detection times, to hopefully reduce overflows.

ML 3.24e
Added 1 more detection check for M4-Script.

pre ML 3.24e
Added alot of stuff, changed some, and removed bugs.
