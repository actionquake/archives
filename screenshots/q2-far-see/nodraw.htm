How do you do it??
I'll tell you.

First, you have to have your sky rotate so fast, it gives you the Hall Of Mirrors effect. That lets you see far, but the sky is screwed up and makes it difficult to hit someone if they are against the HOM effect. So what do you do? Get rid of the effect, type:

gl_clear "1"

In the console. Now you have a pretty pink sky like in the screenshots above. For those of you who want an easy alias, here's mine.

//far see
gl_clear 1
alias sky_t "skyoff"
alias skyoff "alias sky_t skyon; sky stars 99999999999999999999"
alias skyon "alias sky_t skyoff; sky stars"
bind d sky_t

And that, is how you get no draw-in, in quake2 in OpenGL.

I came up with this on my own, and has served me well ever since.
